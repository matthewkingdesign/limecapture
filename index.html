Events are more than a day

Producing digital content can be a costly and timely process. At Lime Capture we see events as content producing machines, continuously providing moments centred around inspiration and connectivity. All there is to do is repurpose the magic.. Thats where we come in. Lime Capture has everything you need to capture the essence of your event and help you spread the message beyond the day.

By creating a podcast series from your event (and sharing it!), your adding real value to your brand and community experience.

Lets make a plan

Before your event we offer a pre-production consultation during which we can discuss style, sponsor opportunities, session content and in general collaborate to get a capture plan for the day.

With the capture plan in place we will capture the sessions, interviews and presenter stings that have been chosen for your podcast.

Post Production

With over 12 years of experience in both pre recorded and live sound engineering, we will carefully listen to and craft your podcast series to have maximin impact, in the smoothest way possible. After compiling your podcast, we will engineer and master to MP3 or WAV format at podcast industry standards.

Distribution

At Limecapture we assist in setting up your podcast hosting service, walking you through submissions to all relevant podcast directories such as Apple Podcasts®, Spotify®, and Google Podcasts®.
